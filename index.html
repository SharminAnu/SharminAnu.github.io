Water Quality Monitoring Web Interface


The quality of drinking water is very crucial for a community to thrive. Lots of US households still suffer from contaminated drinking water like lead, arsenic etc. It is hard to keep track of the water contamination level in a city with complex water pipe structures and reservoirs. Our project will help to tackle this important problem.
